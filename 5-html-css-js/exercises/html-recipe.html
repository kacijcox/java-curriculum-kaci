high-protein custard

this recipe is for a high-protein snack which results in a flan/custard texture. great for breakfast meal prep.

    ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 5 tbsp of cornstarch
  • 2 cups of plain greek yogurt
  • 3 caps vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup stevia in the raw
  • generous amount cinnamon

    • steps

    1. whisk the ingredients together
    2. pour into a square pan with parchment paper lining it
    3. bake for 1 hour, 10 minutes at 350 degrees

    can pair with light agave drizzle. i can't really recommend a serving size because i generally eat the entire
    pan in one day. it's a great alternative when you are dieting and are craving sweets. for me, i am able to
    trick myself just enough with the texture and agave, cinnamon.
